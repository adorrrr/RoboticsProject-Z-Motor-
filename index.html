________________ About Z-Motor ________________

Our project focuses on the development of an AI-powered Rescue Bot designed to operate in aquatic environments. Robotics, the interdisciplinary branch of engineering, involves creating intelligent machines capable of performing tasks autonomously. This Rescue Bot uses advanced automation and AI technology, equipped with a camera for human detection, to carry out water-based rescue missions, reducing the need for human intervention in dangerous conditions. By addressing real-world rescue challenges, our bot aims to enhance safety and efficiency in critical situations.
